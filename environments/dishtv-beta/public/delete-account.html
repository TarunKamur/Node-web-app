Want to delete account?

Please note that once the account is deleted,

  • The account will be deleted permanently and cannot be undone howsoever.
  • The data associated with this account will be lost permanently.
  • All your personal details associated with this account shall be deleted permanently.

Steps to delete account

  • You have to go to settings in the app to select delete account
  • Once you proceed with the account deletion, it will ask for OTP/Password verification either to registered email address/mobile number
  • You have to enter the received OTP or Password either to registered email address/mobile number
  • Once you enter the OTP/Password it will ask for final confirmation to delete the account
  • Once you click on yes, the account will be deleted permanently
